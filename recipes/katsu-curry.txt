47. Katsu Curry

Crispy panko-breaded pork or chicken cutlet on rice, drizzled with rich Japanese curry sauce.

Ingredients:
- 2 pork chops or chicken breasts, pounded thin
- 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1 egg, beaten
- Vegetable oil for frying
- For curry sauce: 1 onion diced, 1 carrot diced, 2 cloves garlic, 2 cups chicken broth, 3 curry roux cubes, 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Steamed rice, shredded cabbage, and lemon wedge, for serving

Instructions:
1. Make curry sauce: sauté onion and carrot until soft; add garlic; add broth and simmer 10 minutes; dissolve roux cubes in; stir in soy sauce; keep warm.
2. Season cutlets with salt and pepper; dredge in flour, dip in egg, then coat in panko.
3. Heat 1 inch of oil in a pan to 350°F; fry cutlets 3-4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through.
4. Drain on paper towels; rest 2 minutes then slice on an angle.
5. Serve sliced katsu over rice alongside shredded cabbage.
6. Pour curry sauce over rice and katsu.

Cook time: 35 minutes
Servings: 2
Cuisine: Japanese
Tags: pork, fried, curry, comfort
