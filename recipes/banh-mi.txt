25. Bánh Mì

A Vietnamese sandwich packed with bold flavors — savory pork, pickled vegetables, and fresh herbs.

Ingredients:
- 4 mini baguettes or hoagie rolls
- 300g pork belly or ground pork patties
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tsp five-spice powder
- 1 tbsp sugar
- For pickled vegetables: 1 carrot and 1/2 daikon, julienned; 1/2 cup rice vinegar; 2 tbsp sugar; 1 tsp salt
- Mayonnaise
- Sriracha or sliced jalapeños
- Fresh cilantro and cucumber slices

Instructions:
1. Pickle carrot and daikon: combine with vinegar, sugar, and salt; let sit at least 30 minutes.
2. Marinate pork in soy sauce, fish sauce, five-spice, and sugar for 30 minutes.
3. Grill or pan-fry pork over medium-high heat until cooked through and slightly charred.
4. Slice baguettes lengthwise; toast lightly.
5. Spread both sides with mayonnaise; layer in pork, pickled vegetables, cucumber, and cilantro.
6. Finish with a drizzle of sriracha and serve immediately.

Cook time: 30 minutes (plus pickling)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Vietnamese
Tags: sandwich, pork, bright-flavors, street-food
