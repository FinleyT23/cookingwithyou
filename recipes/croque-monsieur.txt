78. Croque Monsieur

The ultimate French ham and cheese toasted sandwich, bathed in béchamel and broiled golden.

Ingredients:
- 8 slices thick white bread
- 200g good-quality ham, thinly sliced
- 150g Gruyère cheese, grated
- For béchamel: 2 tbsp butter, 2 tbsp flour, 1 cup whole milk, pinch of nutmeg, salt, white pepper
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- Butter for spreading

Instructions:
1. Make béchamel: melt butter, whisk in flour, cook 1 minute; gradually add milk; simmer until thick; season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
2. Butter one side of each bread slice; flip and spread béchamel on the unbuttered sides.
3. Add Dijon mustard to half the slices; layer with ham and half the Gruyère; close sandwiches.
4. Toast sandwiches in a skillet or oven at 375°F until golden on both sides.
5. Spread remaining béchamel over the top of each sandwich; sprinkle with remaining Gruyère.
6. Broil for 3-4 minutes until bubbling and golden brown.

Cook time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: French
Tags: sandwich, cheese, ham, brunch
