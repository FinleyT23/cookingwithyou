Lemon Garlic Chicken Thighs

A simple, hearty weeknight dinner with bright flavors.

Ingredients:
- 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 lemons (zest and juice)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Mix olive oil, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
3. Coat chicken thighs thoroughly in the mixture.
4. Place in a baking dish skin-side up and roast for 35-40 minutes until golden and cooked through.
5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with roasted vegetables or rice.

Cook time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Mediterranean
Tags: chicken, weeknight, gluten-free, dairy-free
