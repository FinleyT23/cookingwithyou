5. Lemon Herb Quinoa Salad

Light, refreshing, and holds up great for weekly meal prep.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup quinoa, cooked
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
- 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup canned chickpeas, rinsed

Instructions:
1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let cool.
2. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, parsley, and chickpeas.
3. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a small jar.
4. Pour dressing over the salad and toss thoroughly to coat.
5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Cook time: 15 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Mediterranean
Tags: vegan, gluten-free, meal-prep
