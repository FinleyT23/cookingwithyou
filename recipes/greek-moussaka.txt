8. Greek Moussaka

Layers of spiced ground lamb, roasted eggplant, and creamy béchamel baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs ground lamb or beef
- 2 large eggplants, sliced 1/2 inch thick
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp allspice
- 1/4 cup red wine
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- For béchamel: 4 tbsp butter, 1/4 cup flour, 2 cups milk, 2 egg yolks, pinch of nutmeg

Instructions:
1. Salt eggplant slices and let sit 20 minutes; rinse, pat dry, and roast at 400°F for 20 minutes.
2. Brown meat with onion and garlic; add wine, tomatoes, cinnamon, and allspice; simmer 20 minutes.
3. Make béchamel: melt butter, whisk in flour, then gradually add milk; cook until thick; temper in egg yolks.
4. Layer eggplant in a greased baking dish, top with meat sauce, then pour béchamel over everything.
5. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes until top is golden.
6. Let rest 15 minutes before slicing.

Cook time: 1.5 hours
Servings: 6
Cuisine: Greek
Tags: baked, hearty, lamb, Mediterranean
