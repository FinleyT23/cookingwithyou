21. Risotto ai Funghi

Creamy Italian mushroom risotto that rewards patience with luxurious texture.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 cups Arborio rice
- 300g mixed mushrooms (porcini, cremini, shiitake), sliced
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 5 cups warm vegetable or chicken broth
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup Parmesan, grated
- 15g dried porcini mushrooms, rehydrated (reserve soaking liquid)
- Fresh thyme
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Sauté fresh mushrooms in olive oil over high heat until golden; season and set aside.
2. Soften onion and garlic in butter; add rice and toast for 2 minutes, stirring.
3. Pour in wine and stir until absorbed; add rehydrated porcini and their strained liquid.
4. Add warm broth one ladle at a time, stirring constantly and waiting for each addition to absorb.
5. After about 18-20 minutes the rice should be creamy and al dente; remove from heat.
6. Stir in remaining butter and Parmesan; top with sautéed mushrooms and thyme.

Cook time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian
Tags: rice, vegetarian, creamy, patient-cook
