100. Tres Leches Cake

A beloved Latin American sponge cake soaked in three milks and topped with whipped cream.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 5 eggs, separated
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup whole milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- For milk soak: 1 can (14oz) sweetened condensed milk, 1 can (12oz) evaporated milk, 1 cup heavy cream
- For topping: 2 cups heavy cream, 3 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:
1. Beat egg yolks with sugar until pale and thick; fold in flour, baking powder, and milk alternately.
2. Whip egg whites to stiff peaks; gently fold into batter.
3. Pour into a greased 9x13 baking dish; bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
4. Cool the cake slightly; poke all over with a fork or skewer.
5. Mix the three milks together; pour slowly over the cake; refrigerate at least 2 hours until fully absorbed.
6. Whip cream with sugar and vanilla to soft peaks; spread over the soaked cake; refrigerate until ready to serve.

Cook time: 1 hour (plus chilling)
Servings: 12
Cuisine: Latin American
Tags: dessert, cake, sweet, crowd-pleaser
