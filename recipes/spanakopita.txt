35. Spanakopita

Greek spinach and feta pie wrapped in shatteringly crispy phyllo pastry.

Ingredients:
- 500g fresh spinach, washed (or 300g frozen, thawed and squeezed dry)
- 300g feta cheese, crumbled
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- Pinch of nutmeg
- 1 package phyllo dough, thawed
- 1/2 cup olive oil or melted butter
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Wilt spinach in a large pan; squeeze out all moisture and chop roughly.
2. Sauté onion and garlic until soft; cool slightly; combine with spinach, feta, eggs, herbs, and nutmeg; season.
3. Brush a 9x13 baking dish with oil; lay 6 sheets of phyllo, brushing each with oil.
4. Spread filling evenly; top with remaining phyllo, brushing each sheet with oil.
5. Score the top into squares and brush with remaining oil.
6. Bake at 375°F for 40-45 minutes until deeply golden; cool slightly before cutting.

Cook time: 1 hour
Servings: 8
Cuisine: Greek
Tags: pastry, vegetarian, spinach, feta
