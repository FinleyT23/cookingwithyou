75. Chicken Tikka

Juicy Indian spiced chicken skewers with a yogurt marinade, cooked until charred and smoky.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs, cut into 2-inch cubes
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt
- Mint chutney, sliced onions, and lime wedges, for serving

Instructions:
1. Whisk yogurt, lemon juice, oil, garlic, ginger, and all spices together.
2. Add chicken and coat thoroughly; marinate at least 2 hours, ideally overnight.
3. Thread chicken onto skewers; shake off excess marinade.
4. Grill over very high heat for 12-15 minutes, turning, until charred in spots and cooked through.
5. Alternatively, broil on a foil-lined sheet pan, turning once, for similar results.
6. Serve with mint chutney, raw onion rings, and lime.

Cook time: 20 minutes (plus marinating)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Indian
