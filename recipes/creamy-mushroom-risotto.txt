2. Creamy Mushroom Risotto

An elegant, earthy Italian classic that feels like a warm hug.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 cups arborio rice
- 1 lb mixed mushrooms, sliced
- 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth, warm
- 1/2 cup dry white wine
- 2 shallots, minced
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:
1. Sauté mushrooms in olive oil until browned; remove and set aside.
2. In the same pan, melt butter and sauté shallots until translucent.
3. Add rice and toast for 2 minutes, then deglaze with white wine.
4. Add warm broth one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until liquid is absorbed.
5. Fold in mushrooms, remaining butter, and Parmesan once rice is tender and creamy.

Cook time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian
Tags: vegetarian, comfort-food, gourmet
