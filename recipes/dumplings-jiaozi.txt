79. Dumplings (Jiaozi)

Hand-folded Chinese dumplings with a juicy pork and cabbage filling.

Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3/4 cup boiling water
- For filling: 300g ground pork, 1 cup Napa cabbage (salted, squeezed dry, chopped), 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp ginger minced, 2 green onions sliced, 1 egg
- Dipping sauce: 3 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp sesame oil, chili oil and garlic to taste

Instructions:
1. Mix flour with boiling water until dough forms; knead until smooth; rest covered for 30 minutes.
2. Combine all filling ingredients; mix vigorously in one direction until paste-like.
3. Roll dough into a log; cut into pieces; flatten each into a thin round with a rolling pin.
4. Place a teaspoon of filling in the center of each wrapper; fold and pleat edges to seal.
5. Pan-fry in a skillet with oil until bottoms are golden; add 1/4 cup water; cover and steam until water evaporates and bottoms are crispy.
6. Mix dipping sauce ingredients and serve alongside dumplings.

Cook time: 1.5 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Chinese
Tags: dumplings, pork, pan-fried, crowd-pleaser
