59. Chana Masala

Hearty Indian spiced chickpeas in a tangy, aromatic tomato gravy.

Ingredients:
- 2 cans (15oz each) chickpeas, drained
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 2 green chiles, sliced
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp dried mango powder (amchur) or lemon juice
- Salt
- Fresh cilantro and lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:
1. Toast cumin seeds in oil until fragrant; add onion and fry until deeply golden.
2. Add garlic, ginger, and green chiles; cook 2 minutes.
3. Stir in all dry spices; fry 1 minute; add tomatoes and cook until thick, about 10 minutes.
4. Mash a few tomato pieces for a textured sauce.
5. Add chickpeas with a splash of water; stir to coat; simmer 15 minutes.
6. Stir in amchur or lemon juice; adjust seasoning; garnish with cilantro and serve with bhatura or rice.

Cook time: 35 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Indian
Tags: chickpeas, vegetarian, vegan, spiced
