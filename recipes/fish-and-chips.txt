60. Fish and Chips

Golden battered cod with thick-cut chips — the great British classic.

Ingredients:
- 4 cod or haddock fillets (6oz each)
- 1 cup flour, plus more for dusting
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup cold beer (lager works best)
- 1 tsp salt
- 4 large Russet potatoes, cut into thick wedges
- Oil for deep-frying
- Malt vinegar, tartar sauce, and mushy peas, for serving

Instructions:
1. Par-cook potato chips in 325°F oil for 5-6 minutes until soft but not colored; drain and refrigerate.
2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt; whisk in cold beer until just combined (small lumps are fine); refrigerate batter.
3. Heat oil to 375°F; fry chips a second time for 3-4 minutes until golden and crispy; drain and season with salt; keep warm.
4. Dust fish in flour; dip in cold batter and allow excess to drip off.
5. Fry battered fish in 375°F oil for 5-6 minutes, turning once, until deep golden.
6. Drain on paper towels; serve immediately with chips, malt vinegar, and tartar sauce.

Cook time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: British
