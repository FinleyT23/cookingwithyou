36. Beef Rendang

A slow-cooked Indonesian dry curry with intensely caramelized coconut and spice.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs beef chuck, cut into large cubes
- 2 cans (14oz each) coconut milk
- 4 dried chiles or 2 tbsp chili paste
- 6 shallots
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1-inch galangal
- 1-inch ginger
- 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised
- 4 kaffir lime leaves
- 1 turmeric leaf (optional)
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp coriander
- 1 tsp cumin
- Salt and sugar

Instructions:
1. Blend chiles, shallots, garlic, galangal, ginger, turmeric, coriander, and cumin into a paste.
2. Fry paste in a wok over medium heat until very fragrant, about 10 minutes.
3. Add beef, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and coconut milk; bring to a boil.
4. Reduce to a low simmer and cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, for 3-4 hours.
5. As liquid evaporates, the curry will turn darker and drier; keep stirring to prevent burning.
6. When beef is coated in a thick, dark caramelized paste, it is ready; serve over rice.

Cook time: 4 hours
Servings: 6
Cuisine: Indonesian
Tags: beef, slow-cook, spiced, dry-curry
