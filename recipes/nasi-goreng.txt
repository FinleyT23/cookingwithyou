61. Nasi Goreng

Indonesia's national fried rice — dark, smoky, and deeply savory with kecap manis.

Ingredients:
- 3 cups cooked jasmine rice (day-old preferred)
- 2 eggs
- 100g shrimp or chicken, cooked
- 3 tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 3 shallots, thinly sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 red chiles, sliced
- 1 tsp shrimp paste (terasi)
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Cucumber slices, fried shallots, and a fried egg, for serving

Instructions:
1. Sauté shallots, garlic, chiles, and shrimp paste in oil over high heat until very fragrant.
2. Add protein and stir-fry 1-2 minutes.
3. Push to the side; scramble eggs in the center; toss with protein.
4. Add rice and stir-fry vigorously; break up any clumps.
5. Pour kecap manis, soy sauce, and oyster sauce over rice; toss until rice is evenly dark and coated.
6. Serve topped with a fried egg, cucumber, and fried shallots.

Cook time: 15 minutes
Servings: 2
Cuisine: Indonesian
Tags: fried rice, quick, sweet-savory, one-pan
