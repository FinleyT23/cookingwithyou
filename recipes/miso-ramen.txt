4. Miso Ramen

A warming, umami-packed Japanese noodle soup with a deeply flavored broth.

Ingredients:
- 4 cups chicken or pork broth
- 3 tbsp white miso paste
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 200g ramen noodles
- 2 soft-boiled eggs, halved
- 100g chashu pork or sliced chicken
- 1 cup corn kernels
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 2 sheets nori
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, grated

Instructions:
1. Sauté garlic and ginger in a pot with a little oil for 1 minute.
2. Whisk miso paste into a splash of warm broth until dissolved; add to pot.
3. Add remaining broth, soy sauce, and sesame oil; bring to a gentle simmer.
4. Cook ramen noodles separately according to package instructions; drain and divide into bowls.
5. Ladle hot broth over noodles.
6. Top with pork, egg, corn, green onions, nori, and a pat of butter.

Cook time: 30 minutes
Servings: 2
Cuisine: Japanese
Tags: soup, noodles, umami, comforting
