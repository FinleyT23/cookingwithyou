45. Chicken Shawarma

Middle Eastern spiced chicken that's caramelized, juicy, and perfect in a wrap.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- Salt and pepper
- For serving: flatbread, garlic sauce or tahini, pickled turnips, tomato, cucumber

Instructions:
1. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and all spices together; toss chicken in marinade.
2. Marinate at least 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.
3. Bake chicken on a sheet pan at 425°F for 25-30 minutes until cooked through and edges are charred.
4. Rest 5 minutes; slice thinly or chop roughly.
5. Warm flatbread on the stovetop or oven.
6. Assemble wraps with chicken, garlic sauce, pickled turnips, tomato, and cucumber.

Cook time: 35 minutes (plus marinating)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Middle Eastern
Tags: chicken, wrap, spiced, street-food
