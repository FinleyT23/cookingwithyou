81. Huevos Rancheros

A vibrant Mexican breakfast of fried eggs on corn tortillas smothered in ranch salsa.

Ingredients:
- 4 large eggs
- 4 corn tortillas
- 1 can (14oz) diced fire-roasted tomatoes
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 jalapeño, diced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt
- For serving: refried beans, sliced avocado, queso fresco, fresh cilantro

Instructions:
1. Sauté onion, garlic, and jalapeño in 1 tbsp oil until soft; add cumin and chili powder.
2. Add tomatoes; simmer 10 minutes until sauce is thick; season with salt; keep warm.
3. Heat tortillas directly over a gas flame or in a dry skillet until charred in spots; keep warm.
4. Fry eggs in remaining oil to your desired doneness.
5. Spread refried beans on each tortilla; top each with a fried egg.
6. Spoon ranchero sauce generously over eggs; garnish with avocado, queso fresco, and cilantro.

Cook time: 25 minutes
Servings: 2
Cuisine: Mexican
Tags: breakfast, eggs, quick, spiced
