56. Hummus

Ultra-smooth, creamy Middle Eastern chickpea dip — so much better homemade.

Ingredients:
- 2 cans (15oz each) chickpeas, drained (reserve liquid)
- 1/2 cup tahini
- 4 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 4 tbsp ice water (or chickpea liquid)
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Paprika, olive oil, and parsley, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Peel chickpeas by rubbing them in a towel — this step is worth it for extra-smooth hummus.
2. Process tahini, lemon juice, and garlic in a food processor for 1 minute until pale.
3. Add chickpeas and blend for 3-4 minutes, scraping down the sides.
4. With the motor running, add ice water gradually until very smooth and creamy.
5. Season with cumin and salt; blend again; taste and adjust lemon and garlic.
6. Spread into a bowl, create a well, and fill with olive oil; dust with paprika and parsley.

Cook time: 15 minutes
Servings: 6
Cuisine: Middle Eastern
Tags: dip, vegetarian, vegan, quick
