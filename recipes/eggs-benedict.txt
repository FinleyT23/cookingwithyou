40. Eggs Benedict

Poached eggs and Canadian bacon on an English muffin, smothered in hollandaise.

Ingredients:
- 4 large eggs
- 4 slices Canadian bacon or ham
- 2 English muffins, split and toasted
- 2 tbsp white vinegar
- For hollandaise: 3 egg yolks, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tbsp water, 125g unsalted butter melted, salt, cayenne

Instructions:
1. Make hollandaise: whisk egg yolks, lemon juice, and water over a double boiler until thickened; slowly drizzle in melted butter while whisking; season with salt and cayenne; keep warm.
2. Warm Canadian bacon in a skillet; set aside.
3. Bring a wide pan of water to a gentle simmer with vinegar; create a gentle swirl.
4. Crack each egg into a small cup and slip into the simmering water; poach for 3-4 minutes until whites are set but yolks still runny.
5. Remove eggs with a slotted spoon; drain on paper towels.
6. Assemble: top each muffin half with a slice of Canadian bacon and a poached egg; spoon hollandaise generously over the top; serve immediately.

Cook time: 25 minutes
Servings: 2
Cuisine: American
Tags: brunch, eggs, hollandaise, classic
