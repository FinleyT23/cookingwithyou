44. Classic Ceviche

Fresh fish "cooked" in citrus juice with crisp onion, cilantro, and chile.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb fresh white fish (sea bass, snapper, or halibut), cut into 1/2-inch cubes
- 1 cup fresh lime juice (about 8 limes)
- 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 red onion, very thinly sliced
- 1-2 aji amarillo or serrano chiles, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- Salt
- For serving: tortilla chips or tostadas, sliced avocado, sweet potato

Instructions:
1. Place fish in a glass bowl; season generously with salt.
2. Pour lime and lemon juice over fish; ensure fish is submerged; cover and refrigerate for 15-25 minutes until fish turns opaque.
3. Soak red onion slices in cold salted water for 10 minutes to mellow the bite; drain.
4. Drain most of the marinade (leche de tigre) from the fish, leaving a few tablespoons.
5. Toss fish with onion, chiles, and cilantro; taste and adjust salt.
6. Serve immediately on tostadas with avocado and sweet potato.

Cook time: 25 minutes (citrus marinating)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Peruvian
Tags: seafood, fresh, no-cook, bright
