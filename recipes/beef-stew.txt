4. Classic Beef Stew

Deep, rich flavors that develop beautifully over a slow simmer.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs beef chuck, cubed
- 4 carrots, chopped
- 3 potatoes, cubed
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cups beef broth
- 1 cup red wine
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme
- 2 tbsp flour

Instructions:
1. Coat beef in flour and brown in a heavy pot with oil; remove beef.
2. Sauté onions and carrots; stir in tomato paste and deglaze with red wine.
3. Return beef to pot, add broth, potatoes, and thyme.
4. Cover and simmer on low heat for 2 hours until beef is fork-tender.
5. Remove thyme sprigs and serve hot with crusty bread.

Cook time: 2.5 hours
Servings: 6
Cuisine: American
Tags: hearty, slow-cook, dairy-free
