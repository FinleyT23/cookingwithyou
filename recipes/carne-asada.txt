49. Carne Asada

Citrus- and chile-marinated grilled steak, sliced thin for tacos or burritos.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs flank steak or skirt steak
- 1/4 cup orange juice
- 1/4 cup lime juice
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp oregano
- Salt and black pepper
- Corn tortillas, diced white onion, cilantro, and salsa, for serving

Instructions:
1. Combine citrus juices, garlic, cilantro, soy sauce, oil, and spices to make marinade.
2. Score steak lightly and toss in marinade; marinate 2 hours or overnight.
3. Remove steak from marinade and pat lightly dry; season with salt.
4. Grill over very high heat for 4-6 minutes per side for medium-rare; do not overcook skirt steak.
5. Rest 10 minutes; slice thinly against the grain.
6. Serve in warm corn tortillas with onion, cilantro, and salsa.

Cook time: 20 minutes (plus marinating)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Mexican
Tags: beef, grilled, tacos, citrus
