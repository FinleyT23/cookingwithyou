99. Braised Beef Short Ribs

Meltingly tender short ribs slow-braised in red wine until deeply rich and saucy.

Ingredients:
- 4 bone-in beef short ribs (about 3 lbs total)
- 1 bottle red wine (Cabernet or Merlot)
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 carrots, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 cups beef broth
- 2 sprigs rosemary, 3 sprigs thyme, 2 bay leaves
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 tbsp flour
- Salt and black pepper
- Mashed potatoes or creamy polenta, for serving

Instructions:
1. Season short ribs heavily with salt and pepper; sear on all sides in a Dutch oven until deeply browned; remove.
2. Sauté onion and carrot until soft; add garlic and tomato paste; cook 2 minutes.
3. Sprinkle flour over vegetables and stir; pour in wine and scrape up browned bits.
4. Add broth and herbs; return short ribs; liquid should come halfway up the ribs.
5. Cover and braise at 325°F for 2.5-3 hours until meat is completely tender and nearly falling from bones.
6. Remove ribs; strain and reduce sauce until slightly thickened; spoon over ribs; serve over mashed potatoes.

Cook time: 3.5 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: American
Tags: beef, braise, red wine, slow-cook
