73. Chicken Tortilla Soup

A bold, smoky Mexican-inspired soup topped with crispy tortilla strips and all the fixings.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs boneless chicken breast
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 can (14oz) black beans, drained
- 1 cup frozen corn
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 jalapeño, diced
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 corn tortillas, cut into strips and baked or fried until crispy
- Avocado, sour cream, cheddar, and lime, for serving

Instructions:
1. Sauté onion, garlic, and jalapeño in olive oil until soft; add spices and cook 1 minute.
2. Pour in tomatoes and broth; add chicken breasts whole.
3. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
4. Remove chicken and shred with two forks; return to soup.
5. Add black beans and corn; simmer 10 more minutes; adjust seasoning.
6. Serve topped with tortilla strips, avocado, sour cream, cheese, and lime.

Cook time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Mexican-American
Tags: soup, chicken, spicy, one-pot
