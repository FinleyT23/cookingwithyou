43. Saag Paneer

Cubes of golden-fried cheese nestled in a silky, spiced spinach sauce.

Ingredients:
- 400g paneer, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 500g fresh spinach
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 2 green chiles, sliced
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 1/2 cup heavy cream or yogurt
- 3 tbsp ghee or oil
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- Salt

Instructions:
1. Blanch spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes; drain, cool, and blend to a smooth purée.
2. Fry paneer cubes in 1 tbsp ghee until golden on all sides; set aside.
3. In remaining ghee, toast cumin seeds; sauté onion until golden; add garlic, ginger, and chiles.
4. Stir in dry spices and cook 1 minute; add tomatoes and cook down 10 minutes.
5. Add spinach purée; simmer 10 minutes; stir in cream and season.
6. Fold in paneer and warm through; serve with naan or rice.

Cook time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Indian
Tags: vegetarian, spinach, cheese, curry
