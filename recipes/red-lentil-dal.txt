7. Red Lentil Dal

A fragrant, protein-rich staple that is incredibly budget-friendly.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 can coconut milk
- 3 cups water

Instructions:
1. In a large pot, sauté onion and cumin seeds until the onion is golden.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and tomatoes; cook until tomatoes soften.
3. Stir in rinsed lentils and water; bring to a boil then simmer for 20 minutes.
4. Stir in coconut milk and simmer for another 5 minutes until creamy.
5. Serve warm over basmati rice or with toasted naan bread.

Cook time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Indian
Tags: vegan, gluten-free, budget-friendly
