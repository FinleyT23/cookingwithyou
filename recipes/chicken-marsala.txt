12. Chicken Marsala

Pan-seared chicken in a rich Marsala wine and mushroom sauce.

Ingredients:
- 4 boneless chicken breasts, pounded thin
- 1 cup cremini mushrooms, sliced
- 3/4 cup Marsala wine
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 cup flour
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Season chicken with salt and pepper; dredge lightly in flour.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high; sear chicken 4 minutes per side; remove and set aside.
3. Add butter and mushrooms to the same pan; sauté until browned.
4. Add garlic and cook 1 minute; deglaze with Marsala and let reduce by half.
5. Pour in broth and simmer 5 minutes until slightly thickened.
6. Return chicken to pan, spoon sauce over top, and simmer 5 minutes; garnish with parsley.

Cook time: 35 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian-American
Tags: chicken, wine-sauce, mushrooms, elegant
