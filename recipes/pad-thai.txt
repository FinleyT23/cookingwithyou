7. Pad Thai

The iconic Thai stir-fried noodle dish with a perfect balance of sweet, sour, and savory.

Ingredients:
- 200g flat rice noodles
- 200g shrimp or chicken, peeled/sliced
- 2 eggs
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp tamarind paste
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 1 cup bean sprouts
- 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, crushed
- Lime wedges and chili flakes, for serving

Instructions:
1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 30 minutes; drain.
2. Mix fish sauce, tamarind paste, and sugar in a bowl to make the sauce.
3. Heat oil in a wok over high heat; stir-fry garlic and protein for 2-3 minutes.
4. Push to the side, scramble eggs in the center, then toss everything together.
5. Add noodles and sauce; toss and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until noodles are tender.
6. Add bean sprouts and green onions; toss briefly and serve topped with peanuts and lime.

Cook time: 20 minutes (plus soaking)
Servings: 2
Cuisine: Thai
Tags: noodles, stir-fry, quick, popular
