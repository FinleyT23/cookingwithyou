3. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Tacos

A vibrant, plant-based meal packed with flavor and texture.

Ingredients:
- 2 large sweet potatoes, cubed
- 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 8 small corn tortillas
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1/4 cup crumbled feta or cotija
- Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Toss sweet potato cubes with oil, cumin, and chili powder; roast for 25 minutes.
3. Warm the black beans in a small pot over medium heat.
4. Char tortillas over an open flame or in a dry pan until warm.
5. Fill tortillas with potatoes and beans; top with avocado, cheese, and lime.

Cook time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Mexican-Inspired
Tags: vegetarian, gluten-free, healthy
