66. Filipino Adobo

The Philippines' national dish — chicken slowly braised in a tangy soy and vinegar sauce.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs bone-in chicken thighs and drumsticks
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup white cane vinegar or white wine vinegar
- 1 whole head garlic, cloves smashed
- 1 tbsp black peppercorns, cracked
- 3 bay leaves
- 1 tbsp sugar (optional, for balance)
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Steamed white rice, for serving

Instructions:
1. Combine chicken with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves in a bowl; marinate 30 minutes.
2. Transfer everything to a large pan; bring to a boil; skim any foam.
3. Reduce heat and simmer covered for 25 minutes, turning chicken halfway through.
4. Remove chicken; strain and reserve sauce.
5. Heat oil in a skillet; fry chicken skin-side down until crispy and deeply browned.
6. Return reserved sauce to the pan; simmer until slightly reduced; pour over chicken; serve with rice.

Cook time: 50 minutes (plus marinating)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Filipino
Tags: chicken, tangy, soy, slow-braise
