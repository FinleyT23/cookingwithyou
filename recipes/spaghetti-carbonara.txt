1. Spaghetti Carbonara

A silky Roman pasta made rich with eggs, cheese, and pancetta.

Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti
- 150g pancetta or guanciale, diced
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 100g Pecorino Romano, grated
- 50g Parmesan, grated
- Black pepper, freshly cracked
- Salt

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente; reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. Fry pancetta in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy; remove from heat.
3. Whisk egg yolks, whole egg, and both cheeses together in a bowl.
4. Add hot pasta to the skillet with pancetta and toss off the heat.
5. Pour egg mixture over pasta, adding pasta water a splash at a time to create a creamy sauce.
6. Season generously with black pepper and serve immediately.

Cook time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian
Tags: pasta, quick, creamy, classic
