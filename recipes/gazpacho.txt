19. Gazpacho

A refreshing cold Spanish tomato soup made from nothing but raw, ripe vegetables.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs ripe tomatoes, roughly chopped
- 1 English cucumber, roughly chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, roughly chopped
- 1/2 small red onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 3 tbsp sherry vinegar
- 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 2 slices stale bread, torn (optional, for thickness)
- Ice water, as needed

Instructions:
1. Combine tomatoes, cucumber, pepper, onion, and garlic in a blender.
2. Add vinegar, olive oil, salt, and cumin; blend until very smooth.
3. Add bread if using and blend again; thin with ice water to desired consistency.
4. Season with more salt and vinegar to taste.
5. Press through a fine mesh strainer for an extra-smooth texture, if desired.
6. Chill for at least 2 hours; serve cold with a drizzle of olive oil and diced vegetables on top.

Cook time: 15 minutes (plus chilling)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Spanish
Tags: soup, cold, vegan, summer
