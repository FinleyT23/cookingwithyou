37. Minestrone

A hearty Italian vegetable soup that changes with the seasons and gets better the next day.

Ingredients:
- 1 can (15oz) cannellini beans, drained
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 2 zucchini, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup green beans, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 cup small pasta (ditalini or elbow)
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Parmesan rind (optional, for simmering)
- Fresh basil and grated Parmesan, for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large pot; sauté onion, carrot, and celery until soft.
2. Add garlic and tomato paste; cook 2 minutes; add diced tomatoes and broth.
3. Add zucchini, green beans, herbs, and Parmesan rind; bring to a simmer.
4. Cook 20 minutes until vegetables are tender; add cannellini beans and pasta.
5. Simmer until pasta is al dente, about 8 minutes; remove Parmesan rind.
6. Finish with fresh basil; serve with grated Parmesan and crusty bread.

Cook time: 45 minutes
Servings: 6
Cuisine: Italian
Tags: soup, vegetarian, hearty, beans
