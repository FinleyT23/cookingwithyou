83. Avgolemono (Greek Lemon Rice Soup)

A silky, bright Greek soup thickened with egg and lemon — comfort in a bowl.

Ingredients:
- 6 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
- 1/2 cup Arborio or long-grain rice
- 3 eggs
- 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice (about 2 lemons)
- Salt and white pepper
- Fresh dill and lemon slices, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Bring chicken broth to a gentle boil in a large pot.
2. Add rice and simmer for 20 minutes until cooked through.
3. Whisk eggs in a bowl until frothy; whisk in lemon juice until well combined.
4. Ladle 1 cup of hot broth into the egg mixture slowly while whisking (tempering).
5. Pour the tempered mixture back into the pot, stirring gently; do not let it boil.
6. Add shredded chicken and warm through; season with salt and white pepper; serve with fresh dill.

Cook time: 35 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Greek
Tags: soup, lemon, rice, comforting
