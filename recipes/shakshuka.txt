5. Shakshuka

Eggs poached in a spiced, garlicky tomato sauce — a one-pan wonder.

Ingredients:
- 6 large eggs
- 1 can (28oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp sugar
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh parsley and feta cheese, for topping
- Crusty bread, for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; sauté onion and pepper until soft.
2. Add garlic and spices; cook 1 minute until fragrant.
3. Pour in tomatoes and sugar; season with salt and pepper and simmer 10 minutes.
4. Make 6 wells in the sauce and crack an egg into each.
5. Cover and cook on low heat for 8-10 minutes until whites are set but yolks still runny.
6. Top with crumbled feta and parsley; serve straight from the pan with bread.

Cook time: 30 minutes
Servings: 3
Cuisine: Middle Eastern
Tags: eggs, vegetarian, one-pan, spiced
