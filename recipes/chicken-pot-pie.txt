84. Chicken Pot Pie

A flaky, golden pastry lid over creamy chicken and vegetable filling — American comfort food at its finest.

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- 1 cup frozen peas
- 2 carrots, diced and cooked
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1/4 cup flour
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tsp thyme
- Salt and pepper
- 1 sheet store-bought puff pastry or pie crust
- 1 egg, beaten (egg wash)

Instructions:
1. Sauté onion and celery in butter until soft; stir in flour and cook 2 minutes.
2. Gradually whisk in broth and cream; simmer until thick and smooth; add thyme.
3. Stir in chicken, peas, and cooked carrots; season well with salt and pepper.
4. Pour filling into a 9-inch pie dish or individual ramekins.
5. Lay pastry over the top; trim excess; crimp edges; brush with egg wash; cut a few slits.
6. Bake at 400°F for 25-30 minutes until pastry is deeply golden.

Cook time: 1 hour
Servings: 6
Cuisine: American
Tags: chicken, pot-pie, comfort, pastry
