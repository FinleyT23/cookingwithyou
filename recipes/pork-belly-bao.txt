76. Pork Belly Bao

Soft steamed bao buns filled with melt-in-your-mouth braised pork belly and tangy pickles.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb pork belly, skin-on
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp five-spice powder
- 1 cup water
- 8 store-bought steamed bao buns (frozen, reheated)
- For garnish: hoisin sauce, quick-pickled cucumber, sliced green onion, crushed peanuts, cilantro

Instructions:
1. Blanch pork belly in boiling water for 5 minutes; drain and pat dry.
2. Slice into 1.5-inch thick pieces; sear in a skillet until golden on both sides.
3. Combine soy sauces, wine, sugar, five-spice, and water in a pot; add pork; bring to a simmer.
4. Cover and braise on low for 1.5 hours until very tender; increase heat at the end to thicken sauce.
5. Steam bao buns according to package instructions.
6. Open each bun; spread hoisin; layer in pork, pickles, green onion, peanuts, and cilantro.

Cook time: 2 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Chinese
Tags: pork, bao, steamed, street-food
