9. Sesame Ginger Tofu Stir-Fry

A crunchy, savory vegetarian option that beats takeout any night.

Ingredients:
- 1 block extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 1 tbsp cornstarch

Instructions:
1. Toss tofu cubes in cornstarch and pan-fry in oil until all sides are crispy.
2. Remove tofu; in the same pan, stir-fry broccoli and peppers until tender-crisp.
3. Whisk soy sauce, sesame oil, maple syrup, and ginger in a small bowl.
4. Return tofu to the pan, pour in the sauce, and toss until coated and thickened.
5. Serve immediately over white rice or rice noodles.

Cook time: 25 minutes
Servings: 3
Cuisine: Asian-Inspired
Tags: vegan, dairy-free, healthy
