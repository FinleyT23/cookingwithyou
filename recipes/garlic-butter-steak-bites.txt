6. Garlic Butter Steak Bites

A high-protein favorite that feels like a steakhouse treat at home.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs sirloin steak, cubed
- 4 tbsp butter
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Season steak cubes generously with salt and pepper.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over high heat until smoking.
3. Sear steak in batches for 2 minutes per side until browned; remove from pan.
4. Reduce heat to medium; add butter and garlic, cooking until fragrant.
5. Toss steak back into the pan to coat in garlic butter and garnish with parsley.

Cook time: 15 minutes
Servings: 3
Cuisine: American
Tags: keto, high-protein, quick-dinner
