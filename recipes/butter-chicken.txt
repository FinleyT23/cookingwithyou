9. Butter Chicken

Mildly spiced, creamy, and deeply comforting — India's most beloved chicken curry.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs chicken thighs, cubed
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 can (14oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt

Instructions:
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, 1 tsp garam masala, and turmeric for 30 minutes; broil until slightly charred.
2. Sauté onion in butter until golden; add garlic and ginger, cook 2 minutes.
3. Add remaining spices and cook 1 minute; pour in tomatoes and simmer 15 minutes.
4. Blend sauce smooth; return to pan over medium heat.
5. Stir in cream and add chicken; simmer 10 minutes.
6. Serve over basmati rice with naan bread.

Cook time: 50 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Indian
Tags: creamy, mild, chicken, curry
