33. Crispy Beef Tacos

Seasoned ground beef in crispy shells with all the classic toppings.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp oregano
- 1/2 cup water
- 8 hard taco shells
- Shredded lettuce, diced tomato, shredded cheddar, sour cream, and hot sauce, for serving

Instructions:
1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it up as it cooks; drain excess fat.
2. Stir in all spices and water; simmer 5 minutes until sauce thickens and coats the beef.
3. Taste and adjust seasoning.
4. Warm taco shells in the oven at 350°F for 5 minutes until crispy.
5. Fill shells with beef, then layer on desired toppings.
6. Serve immediately.

Cook time: 20 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Mexican-American
Tags: tacos, beef, quick, family-friendly
