93. Sopa de Lima

A bright, citrusy Yucatecan chicken soup with lime and fried tortilla strips.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs bone-in chicken thighs
- 6 cups chicken broth
- 3 limes, juiced (Yucatecan lima preferred if available)
- 1/2 lime zest
- 1 onion, diced (half for broth, half for garnish)
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 jalapeño or habanero, left whole
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp oregano
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil
- 4 corn tortillas, cut into strips and fried
- Fresh cilantro and avocado, for serving

Instructions:
1. Simmer chicken in broth with half the onion, garlic, jalapeño, and oregano for 25 minutes; remove chicken and shred.
2. Strain the broth and return to pot.
3. Sauté remaining onion and tomatoes in oil until soft; add to broth.
4. Add lime juice and zest; return shredded chicken; simmer 10 minutes.
5. Taste and adjust lime and salt; the soup should be notably bright and citrusy.
6. Ladle into bowls over fried tortilla strips; garnish with cilantro and avocado.

Cook time: 50 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Mexican
Tags: soup, chicken, citrus, bright
