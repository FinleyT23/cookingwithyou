57. Korean Fried Chicken

Double-fried chicken with an impossibly crispy coating and sweet-spicy gochujang glaze.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs chicken wings or drumettes
- 1 cup cornstarch
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper
- Oil for deep-frying
- For sauce: 3 tbsp gochujang, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp honey, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 3 cloves garlic minced, 1 tsp ginger minced
- Sesame seeds and sliced green onions, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Pat chicken very dry; season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
2. Whisk together cornstarch, flour, and baking powder; dredge chicken thoroughly.
3. Fry at 320°F for 10 minutes; remove and rest on a rack for 10 minutes.
4. While resting, combine all sauce ingredients in a small saucepan; simmer 3 minutes until slightly thickened.
5. Double-fry chicken at 375°F for 3-4 minutes until deeply golden and shatteringly crispy.
6. Immediately toss in sauce; plate and garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.

Cook time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Korean
Tags: chicken, fried, spicy, crispy
