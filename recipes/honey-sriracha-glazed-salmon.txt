1. Honey Sriracha Glazed Salmon

A perfect balance of sweet and heat for a quick, healthy dinner.

Ingredients:
- 4 salmon fillets
- 3 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp sriracha
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Whisk honey, sriracha, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and lime juice in a bowl.
3. Place salmon on a lined baking sheet and brush half the glaze onto the fillets.
4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
5. Brush with remaining glaze and top with sesame seeds before serving.

Cook time: 20 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Fusion
Tags: seafood, spicy, quick-dinner, gluten-free
