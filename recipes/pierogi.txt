71. Pierogi

Polish filled dumplings boiled until tender, then pan-fried in butter with caramelized onions.

Ingredients:
- For dough: 2 cups flour, 1/2 cup sour cream, 1 egg, 1/2 tsp salt
- For filling: 2 cups mashed potato, 1 cup cheddar or farmer's cheese, 1 onion caramelized, salt and pepper
- 3 tbsp butter for frying
- Sour cream and fresh chives, for serving

Instructions:
1. Mix flour, sour cream, egg, and salt into a smooth dough; rest covered for 30 minutes.
2. Combine mashed potato with cheese, caramelized onion, salt, and pepper for filling.
3. Roll dough thin (1/8 inch); cut into 3-inch rounds with a glass or cutter.
4. Place a teaspoon of filling in the center of each round; fold and crimp edges tightly to seal.
5. Boil pierogi in salted water in batches until they float, about 3-4 minutes; drain.
6. Pan-fry boiled pierogi in butter over medium heat until golden and slightly crispy on each side; serve with sour cream and chives.

Cook time: 1.5 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Polish
Tags: dumplings, potato, vegetarian, comfort
