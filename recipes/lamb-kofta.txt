67. Lamb Kofta

Spiced Middle Eastern lamb meatballs grilled on skewers and served with yogurt and flatbread.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs ground lamb
- 1 onion, grated (squeeze out excess liquid)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup fresh mint, finely chopped
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp allspice
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- Salt and black pepper
- For serving: flatbread, cucumber-yogurt sauce, tomato-onion salad, sumac

Instructions:
1. Combine lamb, grated onion, garlic, herbs, and all spices; mix thoroughly with your hands.
2. Refrigerate the mixture for 30 minutes to firm up.
3. Divide into 8-10 portions; form into elongated oval shapes around flat metal skewers.
4. Grill over high heat for 8-10 minutes, turning carefully, until cooked through and well charred.
5. Rest 3 minutes.
6. Serve on flatbread with yogurt sauce, salad, and a sprinkle of sumac.

Cook time: 20 minutes (plus chilling)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Middle Eastern
Tags: lamb, grilled, skewers, spiced
