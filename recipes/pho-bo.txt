27. Pho Bò

A deeply aromatic Vietnamese beef noodle soup with a crystal-clear, spiced broth.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs beef bones (knuckles and oxtail)
- 300g beef sirloin, very thinly sliced
- 200g flat rice noodles (bánh phở)
- 1 large onion, halved
- 3-inch piece ginger, halved lengthwise
- 3 star anise
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 4 cloves
- 1 tsp coriander seeds
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- Salt
- For serving: bean sprouts, fresh basil, lime, sliced chiles, hoisin, sriracha

Instructions:
1. Blanch beef bones in boiling water for 10 minutes; drain and rinse.
2. Char onion and ginger over open flame or broiler until blackened; add to a large pot with bones.
3. Toast star anise, cinnamon, cloves, and coriander in a dry pan; add to pot.
4. Cover with 10 cups of water; bring to a boil then simmer uncovered for 3 hours.
5. Strain broth; season with fish sauce, sugar, and salt.
6. Cook noodles; divide into bowls; top with raw beef slices; ladle boiling broth over (it cooks the beef); serve with garnishes.

Cook time: 3.5 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Vietnamese
Tags: soup, noodles, beef, slow-cook
