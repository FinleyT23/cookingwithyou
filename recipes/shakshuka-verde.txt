98. Green Shakshuka

A vivid, herb-forward variation on shakshuka with spinach, leeks, and feta.

Ingredients:
- 6 large eggs
- 300g fresh spinach
- 2 leeks, white and light green parts, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 green chile, diced
- 1/2 cup frozen peas
- 1 cup vegetable broth
- 100g feta cheese, crumbled
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp coriander
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- Fresh dill and mint, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet; sauté leeks over medium heat until soft, about 10 minutes.
2. Add garlic, green chile, cumin, and coriander; cook 2 minutes.
3. Add spinach in batches and stir until wilted; add peas and broth; simmer 5 minutes.
4. Taste and season with salt and lemon juice.
5. Make 6 wells in the green sauce; crack an egg into each; crumble feta over everything.
6. Cook on low heat covered for 8-10 minutes until whites are set; garnish with fresh herbs and serve with bread.

Cook time: 35 minutes
Servings: 3
Cuisine: Middle Eastern
Tags: eggs, vegetarian, one-pan, green
