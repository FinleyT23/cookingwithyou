58. Pasta Puttanesca

A bold, punchy Italian sauce made from pantry staples — olives, capers, anchovies, and tomatoes.

Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti or linguine
- 1 can (28oz) whole peeled tomatoes, crushed by hand
- 6 anchovy fillets
- 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and roughly chopped
- 2 tbsp capers, drained
- 4 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente; reserve 1/2 cup pasta water; drain.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium; add anchovies and garlic; stir until anchovies dissolve.
3. Add red pepper flakes, then tomatoes; simmer 10 minutes until sauce thickens.
4. Stir in olives and capers; simmer 5 more minutes.
5. Add drained pasta to the sauce; toss vigorously, adding pasta water as needed.
6. Season with pepper (the anchovies and capers are salty, so taste before adding salt); finish with parsley.

Cook time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian
