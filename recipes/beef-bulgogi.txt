11. Beef Bulgogi

Korean marinated beef that caramelizes beautifully on the grill or stovetop.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs ribeye or sirloin, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1 Asian pear, grated (or 1/4 cup apple juice)
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
- Vegetable oil for cooking
- Steamed rice and lettuce leaves, for serving

Instructions:
1. Combine soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, garlic, ginger, and grated pear to make marinade.
2. Add beef slices and toss to coat; marinate for 30 minutes or up to overnight.
3. Heat a skillet or grill pan over high heat with a little vegetable oil.
4. Cook beef in a single layer for 2-3 minutes per side until caramelized; work in batches.
5. Transfer to a plate and top with green onions and sesame seeds.
6. Serve with rice and lettuce leaves for wrapping.

Cook time: 20 minutes (plus marinating)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Korean
Tags: beef, grilled, sweet-savory, quick
