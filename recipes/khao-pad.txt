42. Khao Pad (Thai Fried Rice)

A quick and smoky Thai fried rice with just the right wok-tossed flavor.

Ingredients:
- 3 cups cooked jasmine rice (day-old works best)
- 200g shrimp, chicken, or tofu
- 2 eggs
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 1 tomato, cut into wedges
- Lime wedges, cucumber slices, and fresh chiles, for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat a wok over the highest heat possible until smoking; add oil.
2. Stir-fry garlic for 30 seconds; add protein and cook until done.
3. Push to the side; scramble eggs in the center until barely set; toss with protein.
4. Add rice in one layer; let sit for 30 seconds to develop color; toss vigorously.
5. Season with fish sauce, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and sugar; stir-fry 2 minutes.
6. Toss in green onions and tomato; plate and serve with lime, cucumber, and fresh chiles.

Cook time: 15 minutes
Servings: 2
Cuisine: Thai
Tags: fried rice, quick, wok, versatile
