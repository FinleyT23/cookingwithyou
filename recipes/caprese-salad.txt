70. Caprese Salad

A simple Italian summer salad where quality ingredients do all the talking.

Ingredients:
- 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced 1/4 inch thick
- 250g fresh mozzarella (fior di latte or burrata), sliced
- 1 large bunch fresh basil leaves
- 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 tbsp aged balsamic vinegar or balsamic glaze
- Flaky sea salt
- Freshly cracked black pepper

Instructions:
1. Arrange tomato slices on a large platter; they should be room temperature, not cold.
2. Alternate mozzarella slices between and overlapping with the tomato slices.
3. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.
4. Drizzle generously with extra-virgin olive oil.
5. Drizzle balsamic vinegar over the top.
6. Finish with flaky sea salt and a generous crack of black pepper; serve immediately.

Cook time: 10 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian
Tags: salad, no-cook, vegetarian, summer
