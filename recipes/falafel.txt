17. Falafel

Crispy, herby chickpea fritters served with creamy tahini and fresh pita.

Ingredients:
- 2 cups dried chickpeas, soaked overnight (do not use canned)
- 1 small onion, roughly chopped
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 cup fresh parsley
- 1/2 cup fresh cilantro
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1/2 tsp cayenne
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tbsp flour
- Salt
- Oil for frying
- Pita, tahini, tomato, and cucumber, for serving

Instructions:
1. Drain soaked chickpeas; pulse in a food processor with onion, garlic, and herbs until a coarse crumble forms.
2. Add cumin, coriander, cayenne, baking powder, flour, and salt; pulse to combine; do not over-process.
3. Refrigerate the mixture for 30 minutes.
4. Shape into small patties or balls.
5. Deep-fry in 350°F oil for 3-4 minutes until deep golden and crispy; drain on paper towels.
6. Serve in pita with tahini, tomato, and cucumber.

Cook time: 30 minutes (plus soaking and chilling)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Middle Eastern
Tags: vegetarian, fried, chickpeas, street-food
