20. Pulled Pork BBQ

Smoky, slow-cooked pork shoulder that shreds into tender, flavorful strands.

Ingredients:
- 4 lbs pork shoulder (bone-in)
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp cayenne
- 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1 cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
- Brioche buns and coleslaw, for serving

Instructions:
1. Mix all dry spices into a rub; coat pork shoulder generously all over.
2. Refrigerate uncovered overnight for best results.
3. Place pork in a slow cooker with apple cider vinegar; cook on low for 8-10 hours.
4. Remove pork and shred with two forks; discard excess fat and bone.
5. Mix shredded pork with enough cooking juices to keep it moist; stir in BBQ sauce.
6. Pile onto buns and top with coleslaw.

Cook time: 8-10 hours (slow cooker)
Servings: 8
Cuisine: American
Tags: pork, BBQ, slow-cook, crowd-pleaser
