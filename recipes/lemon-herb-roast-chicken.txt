55. Lemon Herb Roast Chicken

A perfectly golden, herb-butter roasted chicken that fills the kitchen with incredible aroma.

Ingredients:
- 1 whole chicken (3.5-4 lbs)
- 4 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lemon, zested and halved
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and black pepper
- 1 head garlic, halved crosswise
- 1 onion, quartered

Instructions:
1. Mix butter with garlic, lemon zest, thyme, rosemary, parsley, salt, and pepper.
2. Pat chicken very dry inside and out; carefully loosen skin over the breast and rub herb butter directly on the meat.
3. Rub remaining butter all over the outside; season generously with salt.
4. Stuff cavity with lemon halves, garlic head, and onion.
5. Tie legs together and tuck wings; roast at 425°F for 50-60 minutes until skin is deep golden and juices run clear.
6. Rest 15 minutes before carving.

Cook time: 1.25 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: American
