13. Vegetable Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice layered with spiced vegetables and caramelized onions.

Ingredients:
- 2 cups basmati rice, soaked 30 minutes
- 1 cup green beans, cut
- 1 carrot, diced
- 1 potato, cubed
- 1/2 cup peas
- 1 large onion, thinly sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1/2 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tbsp ghee or butter
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 4 cardamom pods, 2 bay leaves, 4 cloves
- Saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk
- Fresh mint and cilantro

Instructions:
1. Fry onions in ghee until deeply golden and crispy; remove half for garnish.
2. Add whole spices, garlic, and ginger to the remaining onions; sauté 2 minutes.
3. Add vegetables and cook 5 minutes; stir in yogurt, garam masala, and turmeric.
4. Parboil rice with bay leaves until 70% cooked; drain.
5. Layer half the rice over the vegetables, sprinkle with herbs and saffron milk, then top with remaining rice.
6. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20 minutes; fluff and garnish with crispy onions.

Cook time: 1 hour
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Indian
Tags: rice, vegetarian, aromatic, festive
