72. Seafood Paella

A showstopping Spanish rice dish loaded with saffron-scented seafood.

Ingredients:
- 2 cups Bomba or Arborio rice
- 500g mussels and clams, scrubbed
- 300g shrimp, shell-on
- 200g squid, cut into rings
- 4 cups warm fish or seafood broth
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1/2 tsp saffron threads, dissolved in warm water
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- Lemon wedges and fresh parsley, for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large paella pan; sauté onion, pepper, and garlic until soft.
2. Add tomatoes and paprika; cook until thick; add squid and cook 2 minutes.
3. Pour in broth and saffron water; season and bring to a boil.
4. Sprinkle rice evenly; do not stir; cook 10 minutes on medium-high.
5. Arrange shrimp, clams, and mussels on top; press lightly into rice; cook on low 10-12 minutes until shells open and rice is cooked.
6. Rest 5 minutes covered with foil; discard any unopened shells; serve with lemon and parsley.

Cook time: 50 minutes
Servings: 6
Cuisine: Spanish
Tags: rice, seafood, saffron, one-pan
