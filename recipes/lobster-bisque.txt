10. Lobster Bisque

A luxurious, velvety soup with a deep seafood flavor and a touch of cream and brandy.

Ingredients:
- 2 lobster tails
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 1 carrot, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/4 cup brandy or sherry
- 4 cups seafood or chicken broth
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp cayenne
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh chives, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Simmer lobster tails in salted water for 8 minutes; remove meat and chop; reserve shells.
2. Sauté shells in butter for 5 minutes; add onion, celery, carrot, and garlic; cook until soft.
3. Stir in tomato paste and paprika; deglaze with brandy; add broth and shells; simmer 20 minutes.
4. Strain broth, discarding solids and shells; return liquid to pot.
5. Stir in cream and cayenne; simmer 10 minutes; adjust seasoning.
6. Add lobster meat, warm through, and serve garnished with chives.

Cook time: 1 hour
Servings: 4
Cuisine: American
Tags: seafood, elegant, creamy, special-occasion
