32. Tom Yum Soup

Thailand's legendary hot and sour shrimp soup with lemongrass and kaffir lime.

Ingredients:
- 500g large shrimp, shell-on
- 4 cups chicken broth or water
- 3 stalks lemongrass, bruised and cut into 2-inch pieces
- 5 kaffir lime leaves, torn
- 3 slices galangal or ginger
- 3-5 Thai chiles, bruised
- 200g mushrooms, halved
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp Nam Prik Pao (roasted chili paste)
- 1 tsp sugar
- Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Bring broth to a boil with lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, and chiles; simmer 10 minutes.
2. Add mushrooms and cook 3 minutes.
3. Stir in Nam Prik Pao, fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar.
4. Add shrimp and cook just until pink, 2-3 minutes.
5. Taste and adjust the balance of hot, sour, salty, and sweet.
6. Ladle into bowls; garnish with cilantro; leave aromatics in the bowl but don't eat them.

Cook time: 25 minutes
Servings: 2
Cuisine: Thai
Tags: soup, seafood, spicy, sour
