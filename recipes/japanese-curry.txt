34. Japanese Curry

A mild, slightly sweet curry with tender vegetables and meat over steamed rice.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs chicken thighs or beef chuck, cubed
- 2 onions, sliced
- 2 carrots, cut into chunks
- 2 Yukon Gold potatoes, cubed
- 4 cups chicken or beef broth
- 4 cubes Japanese curry roux (e.g. S&B Golden Curry)
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- Steamed short-grain rice, for serving
- Pickled ginger (gari), for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a heavy pot; sauté onions over medium heat until caramelized, about 15 minutes.
2. Add meat and cook until browned on all sides.
3. Add carrots and potatoes; pour in broth; bring to a boil.
4. Skim foam, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes until vegetables are tender.
5. Break curry roux cubes into the pot; stir until fully dissolved; add soy sauce and Worcestershire.
6. Simmer 10 minutes until thickened; serve over rice with pickled ginger.

Cook time: 1 hour
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Japanese
Tags: curry, mild, comfort, family-friendly
