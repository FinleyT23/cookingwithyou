62. Chicken Cacciatore

Rustic Italian hunter's chicken braised with tomatoes, peppers, olives, and wine.

Ingredients:
- 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
- 1 can (28oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup dry red wine
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
- 2 tbsp capers
- 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh parsley and crusty bread, for serving

Instructions:
1. Season chicken generously; brown in olive oil in a Dutch oven in batches; remove.
2. Sauté onion and peppers in the same pot until softened; add garlic and cook 1 minute.
3. Pour in wine and scrape up any browned bits; cook until reduced by half.
4. Add tomatoes, rosemary, and oregano; return chicken to the pot.
5. Cover and braise on low heat for 45 minutes until chicken is very tender.
6. Stir in olives and capers; cook uncovered 10 more minutes to concentrate sauce; garnish with parsley.

Cook time: 1.25 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian
Tags: chicken, braise, tomato, rustic
