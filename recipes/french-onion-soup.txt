6. French Onion Soup

Sweet caramelized onions in a rich broth, crowned with toasted bread and melted Gruyère.

Ingredients:
- 4 large yellow onions, thinly sliced
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 4 cups beef broth
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 tbsp flour
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 4 thick slices baguette, toasted
- 1.5 cups Gruyère cheese, shredded
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Melt butter in a heavy pot over medium-low heat; add onions and cook, stirring often, for 45 minutes until deeply caramelized.
2. Stir in flour and cook 2 minutes; add wine and scrape up any browned bits.
3. Add both broths, thyme, and bay leaf; simmer 20 minutes.
4. Remove thyme and bay leaf; season with salt and pepper.
5. Ladle soup into oven-safe bowls; float a bread slice on top and pile with Gruyère.
6. Broil for 3-5 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.

Cook time: 1.5 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: French
Tags: soup, comfort, cheesy, slow-cook
