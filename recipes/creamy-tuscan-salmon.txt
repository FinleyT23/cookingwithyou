50. Creamy Tuscan Salmon

Pan-seared salmon in a sun-dried tomato cream sauce with spinach — done in 20 minutes.

Ingredients:
- 4 salmon fillets (6oz each)
- 1 cup baby spinach
- 1/3 cup sun-dried tomatoes, drained and sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1/4 cup chicken broth
- 1/4 cup Parmesan, grated
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
- Red pepper flakes
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Pat salmon dry; season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
2. Sear salmon in olive oil over medium-high heat for 4 minutes per side; remove and set aside.
3. In the same pan, sauté garlic in butter for 1 minute; add sun-dried tomatoes and cook 1 minute.
4. Pour in broth and cream; bring to a simmer; stir in Parmesan and red pepper flakes.
5. Add spinach and stir until wilted; taste and adjust seasoning.
6. Return salmon to the pan, spoon sauce over top, and serve immediately.

Cook time: 20 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian-American
