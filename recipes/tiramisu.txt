51. Tiramisu

Italy's iconic coffee-soaked dessert with clouds of mascarpone cream.

Ingredients:
- 500g mascarpone cheese
- 4 eggs, separated
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 200ml strong espresso or coffee, cooled
- 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (Kahlúa or Tia Maria)
- 1 pack ladyfinger biscuits (savoiardi)
- Cocoa powder, for dusting
- Pinch of salt

Instructions:
1. Beat egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick; fold in mascarpone and vanilla until smooth.
2. Whisk egg whites with a pinch of salt to stiff peaks; gently fold into mascarpone mixture.
3. Combine cooled espresso and coffee liqueur in a shallow bowl.
4. Dip each ladyfinger briefly in the coffee — do not soak — and layer in a 9x13 dish.
5. Spread half the mascarpone cream over the biscuits; repeat layers ending with cream.
6. Dust generously with cocoa powder; refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.

Cook time: 30 minutes (plus chilling)
Servings: 8
Cuisine: Italian
Tags: dessert, no-bake, coffee, classic
