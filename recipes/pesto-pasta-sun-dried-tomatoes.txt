8. Pesto Pasta with Sun-Dried Tomatoes

Bright, punchy flavors with minimal effort for busy nights.

Ingredients:
- 12 oz penne pasta
- 1/2 cup basil pesto
- 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, oil-packed and sliced
- 2 cups baby spinach
- 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
- Shaved Parmesan for garnish

Instructions:
1. Boil pasta in a large pot of salted water until al dente.
2. Reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water, then drain the rest.
3. Return pasta to the pot and stir in pesto, sun-dried tomatoes, and spinach.
4. Toss until the spinach wilts, adding reserved pasta water if needed.
5. Top with toasted pine nuts and shaved Parmesan before serving.

Cook time: 15 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian-American
Tags: vegetarian, quick-dinner
