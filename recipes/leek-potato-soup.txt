94. Leek and Potato Soup

A soothing, velvety classic that's equally good hot or cold (vichyssoise).

Ingredients:
- 4 large leeks, white and light green parts only, cleaned and sliced
- 4 Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and diced
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
- 1 cup heavy cream
- Fresh chives, for garnish
- Salt and white pepper

Instructions:
1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat; gently sauté leeks and onion for 10-12 minutes until very soft but not browned.
2. Add garlic and cook 2 minutes.
3. Add potatoes and broth; bring to a boil; simmer 20 minutes until potatoes are completely tender.
4. Blend soup until very smooth with an immersion blender or in batches in a regular blender.
5. Stir in cream; season with salt and white pepper; reheat gently.
6. Serve hot garnished with fresh chives, or refrigerate until cold to serve as vichyssoise.

Cook time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: French
Tags: soup, vegetarian, creamy, leeks
