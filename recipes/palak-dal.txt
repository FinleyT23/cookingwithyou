48. Palak Dal

A nourishing everyday Indian lentil and spinach soup finished with a sizzling spiced butter.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup red lentils (masoor dal)
- 200g fresh spinach, chopped
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1 green chile, sliced
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 4 cups water
- 1 tsp turmeric
- Salt
- For tadka: 2 tbsp ghee, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp mustard seeds, 2 dried red chiles, 1 tsp chili powder, pinch of asafoetida

Instructions:
1. Rinse lentils and cook with water, turmeric, and salt until completely soft, about 20 minutes.
2. In a separate pot, sauté onion until golden; add garlic, ginger, and green chile.
3. Add tomatoes and cook down 10 minutes; stir into the cooked lentils.
4. Add spinach and simmer until wilted and incorporated, about 5 minutes; adjust consistency with water.
5. Make tadka: heat ghee until shimmering; add cumin and mustard seeds; when they pop, add dried chiles, asafoetida, and chili powder; swirl for 30 seconds.
6. Pour sizzling tadka over dal and serve immediately with rice or roti.

Cook time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Indian
Tags: lentils, vegetarian, healthy, everyday
