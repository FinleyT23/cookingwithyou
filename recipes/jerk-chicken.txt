52. Jerk Chicken

Fiery, smoky Jamaican chicken with a deeply spiced scotch bonnet marinade.

Ingredients:
- 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces (or 2 lbs thighs)
- 3 scotch bonnet or habanero peppers, seeded
- 4 green onions, roughly chopped
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1-inch ginger
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tsp allspice
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp nutmeg
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Blend all ingredients except chicken into a smooth paste.
2. Score chicken pieces deeply; rub jerk paste all over, getting into the cuts.
3. Marinate for at least 4 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
4. Grill chicken over medium-charcoal heat for 40-50 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through and well charred on the outside.
5. Alternatively, roast at 400°F for 45 minutes then finish under the broiler.
6. Rest 10 minutes and serve with rice and peas and festival bread.

Cook time: 1 hour (plus marinating)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Jamaican
Tags: chicken, spicy, grilled, Caribbean
