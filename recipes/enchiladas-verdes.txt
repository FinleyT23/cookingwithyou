28. Enchiladas Verdes

Corn tortillas stuffed with chicken and smothered in a bright, tangy tomatillo salsa.

Ingredients:
- 12 corn tortillas
- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- 1.5 lbs tomatillos, husked and rinsed
- 2 jalapeños or serranos
- 1/2 onion, roughly chopped
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1/2 cup fresh cilantro
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1.5 cups Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt

Instructions:
1. Roast tomatillos, jalapeños, onion, and garlic under the broiler for 8-10 minutes until charred.
2. Blend with cilantro and salt until smooth; taste and adjust seasoning.
3. Warm tortillas in a dry pan; dip each in the green salsa to coat.
4. Fill with shredded chicken, roll tightly, and place seam-side down in a greased baking dish.
5. Pour remaining salsa and sour cream over the top; sprinkle with cheese.
6. Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes until bubbly and cheese is melted.

Cook time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Mexican
Tags: chicken, baked, tomatillo, cheesy
