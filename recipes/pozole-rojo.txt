14. Pozole Rojo

A hearty Mexican hominy soup with tender pork and a deep red chile broth.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs pork shoulder
- 2 cans (15oz each) hominy, drained
- 4 dried ancho chiles, stemmed and seeded
- 2 dried guajillo chiles, stemmed and seeded
- 5 cloves garlic
- 1 onion, quartered
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp oregano
- 6 cups chicken broth
- Salt
- For serving: shredded cabbage, radishes, dried oregano, lime, tostadas

Instructions:
1. Simmer pork with half the onion, 2 garlic cloves, and salt in broth for 1.5 hours until tender.
2. Remove pork, shred, and strain broth; reserve.
3. Toast dried chiles in a dry skillet 1 minute; soak in hot water 20 minutes; drain.
4. Blend chiles with remaining garlic, onion, cumin, oregano, and 1 cup reserved broth until smooth.
5. Fry the chile sauce in a pot for 5 minutes; add remaining broth, hominy, and shredded pork.
6. Simmer 20 minutes; serve hot with all the garnishes.

Cook time: 2.5 hours
Servings: 6
Cuisine: Mexican
Tags: soup, pork, hearty, slow-cook
