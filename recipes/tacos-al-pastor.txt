3. Tacos al Pastor

Smoky, citrus-marinated pork with the sweetness of pineapple on warm corn tortillas.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs pork shoulder, thinly sliced
- 1 cup pineapple chunks
- 3 dried guajillo chiles, rehydrated
- 2 chipotle peppers in adobo
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1/4 cup orange juice
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp oregano
- Corn tortillas, white onion, cilantro, and lime for serving

Instructions:
1. Blend chiles, chipotle, garlic, orange juice, vinegar, cumin, and oregano into a marinade.
2. Coat pork slices in marinade and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
3. Cook pork in a hot cast iron skillet in batches until caramelized and cooked through.
4. In the same pan, char pineapple chunks for 2 minutes.
5. Warm tortillas and layer with pork, pineapple, onion, and cilantro.
6. Serve with lime wedges.

Cook time: 30 minutes (plus marinating)
Servings: 6
Cuisine: Mexican
Tags: tacos, pork, smoky, street-food
