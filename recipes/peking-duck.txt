29. Peking Duck

Lacquered, crackling-skinned roast duck served with thin pancakes and hoisin sauce.

Ingredients:
- 1 whole duck (about 5 lbs)
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 1 tsp five-spice powder
- 1 tsp white pepper
- For serving: Chinese pancakes, hoisin sauce, sliced cucumber, sliced green onions

Instructions:
1. Clean duck; dry thoroughly inside and out; tie off the neck opening.
2. Blanch duck with boiling water poured over all sides; pat dry and air-dry in the fridge uncovered for 24 hours.
3. Mix honey, soy sauce, wine, and spices; brush all over duck; let dry 1 hour.
4. Roast at 350°F breast-side down for 30 minutes; flip and roast 30 more minutes.
5. Increase to 450°F for 10-15 minutes until skin is deeply lacquered and crispy.
6. Rest 10 minutes; carve; serve skin and meat separately with warm pancakes, hoisin, cucumber, and green onions.

Cook time: 1.5 hours (plus 24 hours drying)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Chinese
Tags: duck, roasted, special-occasion, crispy
