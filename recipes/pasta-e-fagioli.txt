53. Pasta e Fagioli

A thick, rustic Italian bean and pasta soup that eats like a complete meal.

Ingredients:
- 1 can (15oz) cannellini beans, drained (reserve liquid)
- 1 can (15oz) borlotti or cranberry beans, drained
- 1 cup ditalini or small pasta
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 3 stalks celery, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 100g pancetta, diced
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 2 sprigs rosemary
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Parmesan rind
- Salt, pepper, and extra-virgin olive oil, for finishing

Instructions:
1. Render pancetta in olive oil; add onion, carrot, and celery; sauté until soft.
2. Add garlic and cook 1 minute; stir in tomatoes and cook 5 minutes.
3. Mash half the cannellini beans; add mashed and whole beans, broth, rosemary, and Parmesan rind.
4. Simmer 20 minutes; remove rosemary and Parmesan rind.
5. Add pasta and cook until al dente; the soup should be very thick.
6. Season well; serve with a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil and grated Parmesan.

Cook time: 45 minutes
Servings: 6
Cuisine: Italian
Tags: soup, beans, pasta, hearty
