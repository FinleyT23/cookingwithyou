10. Sheet Pan Sausage and Peppers

The ultimate "set it and forget it" weeknight dinner savior.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb Italian sausages, sliced into rounds
- 3 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 red onion, sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. On a large sheet pan, toss sausages, peppers, and onions with oil and spices.
3. Spread the mixture into a single, even layer across the pan.
4. Roast for 25-30 minutes, tossing halfway through, until sausages are browned.
5. Serve as-is for a low-carb meal or inside toasted hoagie rolls.

Cook time: 35 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian-American
Tags: sheet-pan, easy-cleanup, gluten-free
