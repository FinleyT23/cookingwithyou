80. Herb-Crusted Lamb Chops

Elegant, quick-cooking lamb chops with a bright mustard and herb coating.

Ingredients:
- 8 lamb loin chops or rib chops
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 2 tbsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and black pepper
- Mint jelly or yogurt sauce, for serving

Instructions:
1. Pat lamb chops dry; season generously with salt and pepper.
2. Sear in a hot oven-safe skillet with olive oil for 2 minutes per side to brown.
3. Mix breadcrumbs, rosemary, thyme, and garlic together.
4. Brush the top of each chop with Dijon mustard; press herb breadcrumb mixture firmly onto the mustard.
5. Transfer to oven; roast at 400°F for 8-10 minutes for medium-rare.
6. Rest 5 minutes; serve with mint jelly or yogurt sauce.

Cook time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: French
Tags: lamb, roasted, elegant, quick
