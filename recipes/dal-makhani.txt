24. Dal Makhani

Silky, slow-cooked black lentils in a rich, buttery tomato sauce.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup whole black lentils (urad dal), soaked overnight
- 1/4 cup red kidney beans, soaked overnight
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1 can (14oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp chili powder
- Salt

Instructions:
1. Pressure cook or boil soaked lentils and kidney beans until very soft, about 45-60 minutes.
2. Melt butter in a heavy pan; add cumin seeds and let sizzle; sauté onion until deeply golden.
3. Add garlic and ginger; cook 2 minutes; stir in chili powder and tomatoes.
4. Simmer tomato mixture 15 minutes until thick; add to cooked lentils.
5. Simmer everything together on very low heat for 30-45 minutes, stirring often.
6. Stir in cream and garam masala; finish with a knob of butter and serve with naan.

Cook time: 2 hours (plus soaking)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Indian
Tags: lentils, vegetarian, creamy, slow-cook
