26. Paella Valenciana

Spain's most iconic rice dish, golden with saffron and cooked in a single wide pan.

Ingredients:
- 2 cups Bomba or short-grain rice
- 500g chicken thighs, bone-in
- 200g rabbit or extra chicken pieces
- 200g green beans, trimmed
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 onion, grated
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 cups warm chicken broth
- 1/2 tsp saffron threads, dissolved in 2 tbsp warm water
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt
- Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large paella pan; brown chicken and rabbit well on all sides; push to edges.
2. Sauté onion and garlic in the center; add tomatoes and cook down until thick.
3. Stir in paprika; add green beans and toss.
4. Pour in broth and saffron water; season well; bring to a boil.
5. Sprinkle rice evenly over the pan; do not stir; cook on medium-high for 10 minutes.
6. Reduce heat and cook 8-10 minutes more until rice has absorbed liquid and a crust (socarrat) forms on the bottom; rest 5 minutes and serve with lemon.

Cook time: 1 hour
Servings: 6
Cuisine: Spanish
Tags: rice, chicken, saffron, one-pan
