2. Chicken Tikka Masala

Tender charred chicken bathed in a luscious, spiced tomato cream sauce.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs chicken breast, cubed
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 can (14oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 2 tbsp butter
- Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, half the spices, and salt for at least 1 hour.
2. Grill or broil chicken until charred; set aside.
3. Sauté onion in butter until golden; add garlic and ginger, cook 2 minutes.
4. Stir in remaining spices, then add crushed tomatoes; simmer 10 minutes.
5. Blend sauce until smooth, return to pan, and stir in cream.
6. Add chicken to sauce and simmer 10 minutes; garnish with cilantro and serve with rice.

Cook time: 45 minutes (plus marinating)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Indian
