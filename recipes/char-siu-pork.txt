74. Char Siu Pork

Cantonese BBQ pork with a lacquered, caramelized crust and tender, juicy interior.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs pork shoulder or pork tenderloin
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp five-spice powder
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Red food coloring (optional, traditional)
- Extra honey for glazing

Instructions:
1. Mix soy sauce, hoisin, honey, Shaoxing wine, oyster sauce, five-spice, sesame oil, and garlic into a marinade.
2. Add food coloring if using; coat pork thoroughly; marinate overnight in the refrigerator.
3. Bring pork to room temperature; place on a rack over a foil-lined baking sheet.
4. Roast at 425°F for 15 minutes; flip and brush with extra honey.
5. Roast another 10-15 minutes until edges are caramelized and a little charred.
6. Rest 10 minutes; slice into pieces; serve with rice or in bao buns.

Cook time: 35 minutes (plus overnight marinade)
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Chinese
Tags: pork, BBQ, sweet-savory, Cantonese
