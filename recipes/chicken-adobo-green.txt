91. Green Chicken Enchiladas with Sour Cream Sauce

Tender chicken enchiladas in a rich, creamy green chile and sour cream sauce.

Ingredients:
- 12 corn tortillas
- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 can (7oz) diced green chiles
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
- 1/2 onion, diced and sautéed
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1.5 cups Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Make sauce: melt butter, whisk in flour; add broth gradually; stir in sour cream, cream cheese, green chiles, garlic powder, and cumin until smooth.
2. Mix shredded chicken with sautéed onion and 1/2 cup of the sauce; season.
3. Warm tortillas in a dry pan; fill each with chicken mixture and roll tightly.
4. Arrange seam-side down in a greased 9x13 baking dish.
5. Pour remaining sauce over tortillas; top with shredded cheese.
6. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes until bubbly and golden.

Cook time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Mexican-American
