95. Lobster Tacos

Sweet, butter-poached lobster in warm tortillas with a light citrus slaw.

Ingredients:
- 2 lobster tails
- 4 tbsp unsalted butter
- 1 clove garlic, smashed
- 8 small corn or flour tortillas
- For citrus slaw: 2 cups shredded cabbage, juice of 1 lime, 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pinch of sugar
- Avocado slices, chipotle mayo (mayo + chipotle in adobo), and fresh cilantro, for serving

Instructions:
1. Remove lobster meat from shells; cut into chunks.
2. Melt butter in a small saucepan with garlic; keep at a very gentle simmer (around 160°F).
3. Add lobster pieces and poach in butter for 5-7 minutes until just cooked through; do not rush this step.
4. Toss slaw ingredients together and set aside.
5. Warm tortillas.
6. Assemble tacos: spread chipotle mayo on each tortilla; top with slaw, lobster, avocado, and cilantro.

Cook time: 25 minutes
Servings: 2
Cuisine: American
Tags: seafood, lobster, tacos, special-occasion
