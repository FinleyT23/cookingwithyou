68. Mushroom Stroganoff

A rich, earthy vegetarian take on the classic Russian stroganoff with a creamy sour cream sauce.

Ingredients:
- 600g mixed mushrooms (cremini, shiitake, oyster), thickly sliced
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 cup vegetable broth
- 2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (use vegan version if needed)
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp paprika
- Fresh dill and parsley
- Egg noodles or mashed potatoes, for serving
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Sauté mushrooms in butter and oil over high heat until deeply browned; season and remove.
2. In the same pan, sauté onion until soft; add garlic and cook 1 minute.
3. Sprinkle flour over onions and stir 1 minute; add broth, mustard, and Worcestershire; simmer until slightly thickened.
4. Return mushrooms to the pan.
5. Remove from heat; stir in sour cream until smooth; add paprika; gently reheat on low (do not boil).
6. Season with salt and pepper; serve over egg noodles topped with fresh dill.

Cook time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Russian
Tags: mushrooms, vegetarian, creamy, comfort
