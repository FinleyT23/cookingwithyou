77. Chilaquiles

A beloved Mexican breakfast of crispy tortilla chips simmered in salsa until just softened.

Ingredients:
- 8 corn tortillas, cut into triangles and fried or baked until crispy
- 2 cups salsa roja or salsa verde (canned or homemade)
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 2 eggs (optional, fried)
- 1/2 cup crumbled queso fresco or cotija
- 1/4 cup sour cream or crema
- Sliced red onion, fresh cilantro, avocado, and lime, for serving

Instructions:
1. Warm salsa and broth together in a large skillet over medium heat; let it simmer gently.
2. Add crispy tortilla chips to the simmering salsa; toss gently to coat.
3. Cook for 2-3 minutes — the chips should soften slightly but still have some texture; do not over-stir.
4. Plate chilaquiles immediately.
5. Top with crumbled cheese, a drizzle of crema, sliced onion, and cilantro.
6. Add a fried egg on top if desired; serve with lime and avocado.

Cook time: 20 minutes
Servings: 2
Cuisine: Mexican
Tags: breakfast, eggs, quick, tortillas
