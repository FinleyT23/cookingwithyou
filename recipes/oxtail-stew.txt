96. Oxtail Stew

Richly braised oxtail that slowly surrenders into the most gelatinous, unctuous comfort stew.

Ingredients:
- 3 lbs oxtail, cut into segments
- 1 bottle red wine
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 carrots, diced
- 3 stalks celery, diced
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cans (14oz each) diced tomatoes
- 2 cups beef broth
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 sprigs thyme, 2 bay leaves
- 2 tbsp flour
- 3 tbsp oil
- Salt and pepper
- Creamy mashed potatoes or polenta, for serving

Instructions:
1. Season oxtail generously with salt and pepper; dredge in flour.
2. Brown oxtail deeply in oil in a Dutch oven, in batches; remove and set aside.
3. Sauté onion, carrot, and celery in the same pot; add garlic and tomato paste.
4. Pour in wine and scrape up all browned bits; add tomatoes, broth, thyme, and bay leaves.
5. Return oxtail; bring to a simmer; cover and braise in a 300°F oven for 3.5-4 hours until meat falls from bone.
6. Remove oxtail; skim fat from sauce; reduce sauce on stovetop if needed; serve with mashed potatoes.

Cook time: 4.5 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: American
Tags: beef, braise, hearty, slow-cook
