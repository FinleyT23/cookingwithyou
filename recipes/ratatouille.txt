41. Ratatouille

A classic Provençal vegetable stew that turns humble garden produce into something special.

Ingredients:
- 1 eggplant, cubed
- 2 zucchini, sliced
- 2 bell peppers (red and yellow), diced
- 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp herbes de Provence
- 1/2 tsp thyme
- Fresh basil, for finishing
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Salt eggplant cubes and let drain 20 minutes; pat dry.
2. Heat olive oil in a wide pot; sauté eggplant until lightly browned; remove.
3. In the same pot, sauté onion and peppers until soft; add garlic and cook 1 minute.
4. Add tomatoes and cook down for 10 minutes; stir in herbs and season.
5. Return eggplant to the pot along with zucchini; cover and cook on low for 20-25 minutes until all vegetables are very tender.
6. Uncover and cook 5 more minutes to concentrate flavors; finish with fresh basil; serve warm or at room temperature.

Cook time: 1 hour
Servings: 4
Cuisine: French
Tags: vegetarian, vegan, slow-cook, Provençal
