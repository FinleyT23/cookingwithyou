15. Salmon Teriyaki

Glazed, caramelized salmon with a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce.

Ingredients:
- 4 salmon fillets (6oz each)
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp mirin
- 2 tbsp sake or dry sherry
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- Sesame seeds and sliced green onions, for garnish
- Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions:
1. Combine soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, and sesame oil in a small saucepan; simmer until slightly thickened; set aside.
2. Pat salmon fillets dry and season lightly with salt.
3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat; sear salmon skin-side up for 3-4 minutes.
4. Flip salmon, brush with teriyaki sauce, and cook 3 more minutes.
5. Brush with sauce once more and let glaze in the pan for 1 minute.
6. Serve over rice, drizzle with remaining sauce, and garnish.

Cook time: 20 minutes
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Japanese
Tags: fish, sweet-savory, quick, healthy
