31. Osso Buco

Braised veal shanks in a white wine and vegetable sauce, finished with bright gremolata.

Ingredients:
- 4 veal shanks, about 1.5 inches thick
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- Flour for dredging
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- For gremolata: zest of 1 lemon, 1 clove garlic minced, 3 tbsp parsley chopped

Instructions:
1. Season and dredge veal shanks in flour; sear in olive oil until browned all over; remove.
2. Sauté onion, carrot, and celery in the same pot until soft; add garlic and tomato paste.
3. Deglaze with white wine; reduce by half; add broth and tomatoes.
4. Return shanks; cover and braise at 325°F for 1.5-2 hours until fall-off-the-bone tender.
5. Mix gremolata ingredients in a small bowl.
6. Serve shanks with braising sauce and a spoonful of gremolata; alongside risotto or polenta.

Cook time: 2.5 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Italian
Tags: veal, braise, elegant, slow-cook
