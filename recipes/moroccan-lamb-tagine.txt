22. Moroccan Lamb Tagine

Fall-apart lamb with warm spices, preserved lemon, and olives in a fragrant sauce.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs lamb shoulder, cut into chunks
- 1 onion, grated
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp ginger
- 1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 cup green olives
- 1 preserved lemon, skin only, sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Fresh cilantro and parsley
- Couscous or flatbread, for serving

Instructions:
1. Toss lamb with all spices and marinate 1 hour or overnight.
2. Brown lamb in olive oil in a tagine or heavy pot; remove.
3. Sauté onion and garlic in the same pot; return lamb; add tomatoes and broth.
4. Cover and cook on very low heat for 1.5-2 hours until lamb is very tender.
5. Stir in olives and preserved lemon; cook 10 more minutes.
6. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve over couscous.

Cook time: 2.5 hours
Servings: 4
Cuisine: Moroccan
Tags: lamb, slow-cook, spiced, North African
